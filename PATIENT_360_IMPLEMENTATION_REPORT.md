# Patient 360 Implementation Report

## Current Experience
- The clinical shell keeps patient search visible in the header for desktop workflows.
- Existing patient lookup and patient detail routes remain the main path into Patient 360.
- Operational users are directed toward patient workflows rather than catalog-style modules.

## Workflow Intent
- Search by mobile, name, UHID, and MRN.
- Open the patient record directly from search results.
- Use the patient record as the central place for visits, history, labs, prescriptions, invoices, and payments.

## Sprint Status
- Workflow surfacing improved through the clinical shell.
- No new patient 360 module was added in this sprint.
